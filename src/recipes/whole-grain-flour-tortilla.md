---
title: Whole grain flour tortilla
ingredients:
  - 125g whole grain flour
  - 125g plain flour
  - 125ml hot water
  - 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  - generous pinch of salt
---

Add all ingredients and knead into a supple dough. When you think you're done,
you're about half way.

Knead a bit more. No really.

Let the dough rest for at least a half an hour in an airtight container, it will
not rise but the moisture in the dough should not be able to escape.

Roll the dough out into 4-6 tortillas. Cook the tortillas in a piping hot cast
iron pan for 45 seconds on each side.

Keep the freshly made tortillas stacked on top of each other under a tea towel
while you prepare the rest of the meal.
