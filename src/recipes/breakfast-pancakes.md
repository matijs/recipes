---
title: Breakfast pancakes
ingredients:
  - 140g plain flour
  - 1 tablespoon of sugar
  - 2 heaped teaspoons of baking powder
  - 140ml full fat milk
  - 15g molten butter (use a microwave)
  - 2 large-ish eggs
---

Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add the other ingredients an whisk into a
thick batter.

Fry the pancakes in a blini pan. Should make about 10 pancakes.
